6 Compliance
6.1 Direct compliance requirements

Vendors shall specify which versions of the X Window System and Motif is supported by their implementation. Vendors shall document the value needed (if any) for &XMUMPS.Exit to work with the underlying operating system. No other requirements are supplied herein.
