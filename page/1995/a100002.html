ANSI/MDC X11.1–1995

Revision of
ANSI/MDC X11.1–1990

American National Standard
for Information Systems –

Programming Languages –
M

Sponsor
MUMPS Development Committee

Approved December 8, 1995
American National Standards Institute, Inc

Abstract

This standard contains a three-section description of various aspects of the M computer programming language. Section 1, the M Language Specification, consists of a stylized English narrative definition of the M language. Section 2, the M Portability Requirements, identifies constraints on the implementation and use of the language for the benefit of parties interested in achieving M application code portability. Section 3 is a binding to ANSI X3.64 (Terminal Device Control Mnemonics).
