7.2.18 UNITS Draw Command

    drawunits::= expr V UNITS , unitspec

The drawunits drawcommand specifies the unit of measure for subsequent drawcommands. If the unit of measure is CHAR, the basis font is the font specified by the most recent set of FFACE, FSIZE and FSTYLE drawcommands. If the unit of measure is REL, the basis size is the size of the gadget at creation.
