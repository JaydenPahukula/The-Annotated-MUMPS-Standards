C.3 Beyond database functions

MUMPS users have often expressed needs for process-to-process communication, remote device support, remote job entry, remote program loading, and other functions available from proprietary protocols. OMI may be an appropriate vehicle for some of these. Its designers will have to balance the virtues of simplicity and functionality.
