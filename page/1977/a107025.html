7.1.3.3 ^$Event

^$E[VENT] ( eventexpr )

    eventexpr::= expr V │
│
│		 "EVENTDEF"
einfoattribute		 │
│
│

Note that einfoattribute is defined in X11.6, the MWAPI standard, along with its semantics. Nodes under ^$Event( einfoattribute ) are used to identify specific behavior of MWAPI events.

Nodes under ^$Event( "EVENTDEF" ) are used to identify specific behavior of the named events.
