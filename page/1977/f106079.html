6.5 Choice Attributes

Choice attributes, identified by cattributes, define the characteristics of choices. For gadgets, choice specifications have the form

^$W[INDOW] ( wname , gkeyword , gname , ckeyword , item , cattribute )

    ckeyword::= expr V CHOICE
