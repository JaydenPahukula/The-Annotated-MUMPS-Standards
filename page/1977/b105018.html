5.3.9.6 Control mnemonic

Produces an effect which is defined by the mnemonic space of the request.

  1. <SI> = 6
  2. <LS> Included in this <LS> are 1 or more strings <LS>. The first is the value of controlmnemonic from WRITE /controlmnemonic. Subsequent ones, if any, are its parameters.
