4.1.11 LINKID dattribute

Definition

LINKID provides, via descendant nodes, information about the data exchange links in which the display is participating. Each link is identified by an entry of the form

^$DI[SPLAY] ( display , linkidkeyword , delinkid )

where

    linkidkeyword::= expr V LINKID

No value is present at the level of the LINKID dattribute itself. For all descendant nodes, the value indicates the role of the M[UMPS] process in the link. This value may not be set, but may be killed. By killing a delinkid subscript an M[UMPS] process closes the corresponding link.
