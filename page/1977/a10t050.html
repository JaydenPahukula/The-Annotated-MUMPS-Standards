intexpr

See intexpr for formal definition.

  1. Result A.
    Take the numeric interpretation of the value in A.
    That is, apply the rules given in Section 3.2.5 of MDC/28 to A.
    Remove any fraction.
    If the result is the null string (""), or "-", 0 A.
    A Result.
