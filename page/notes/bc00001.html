Informative Annex C
Extensions

Plans for future versions of this International Standard, and proposed extensions to the ANSI/MDC X11.1 standard defining the MUMPS language should be of interest to those wishing to implement, use, or extend this International Standard. The MUMPS Development Committee welcomes participation by all who want to contribute.
