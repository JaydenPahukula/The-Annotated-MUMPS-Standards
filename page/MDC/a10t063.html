numlit

See numlit for formal definition.

  1. Period (".") A.
  2. Remove leading zeros from Result.
    If Result > 0, Result A.
  3. Otherwise, zero ("0") A.
  4. Concatenate (A, ".") A.
  5. Remove trailing zeros from Result. Concatenate (A, Result) A.
  6. Concatenate (A, "E") A.
  7. Concatenate (A, "-") A.
  8. Remove leading zeros from Result.
    Concatenate (A, Result) A.
  9. Convert A to a numeric data value, using the algorithm defined in Section 3.2.4.2 of MDC/28.
    Place A Result.
