7.1.3.11 ^$Z[unspecified]

^$Z[unspecified] ( unspecified )

^$Z[unspecified] will provide implementation-specific information. Z is the initial letter for defining non-standard structured system variables. The requirement that ^$Z be used permits the unused initial letters to be reserved for future extensions to the standard without altering the execution of existing programs which observe the rules of the standard.
