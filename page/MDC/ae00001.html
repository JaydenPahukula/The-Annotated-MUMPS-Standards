Annex E: Transportability of M[UMPS] Software Systems (informative)

The transfer of routines between machine environments is affected by numerous machine and operating systems factors. A standard transfer format for both routines and data stored within global variables cannot at the same time easily cope with the simple and the complex case efficiently, in addition to dealing with the environmental idiosyncrasies. Therefore, the responsibility for the detailed format is left to the transferor.
