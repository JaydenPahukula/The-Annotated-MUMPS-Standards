Annex C
(informative)
Extensions

Plans for future versions of this standard, and proposed extensions to the ANSI/MDC X11.1 standard defining the M[UMPS] language should be of interest to those wishing to implement, use, or extend this standard. The MUMPS Development Committee welcomes participation by all who want to contribute.
