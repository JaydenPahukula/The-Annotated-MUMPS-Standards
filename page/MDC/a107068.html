7.1.4.10.11 $Principal

$P[RINCIPAL]

identifies the principal I/O device, which is defined in the following fashion:

  1. If the process is initiated by another M[UMPS] process then $Principal is given the value of $Principal of the initiating process, unless overriden by implementation-specific Job parameters.
  2. If the process is initiated from a specific device then $Principal is given the identifier of that device.
  3. Otherwise $Principal is given an implementation-specific value.

$Principal is constant throughout the active life of a process.
