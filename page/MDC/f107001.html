7. Drawing

A drawing is specified by assigning drawcommand values to immediately descendant nodes of the DRAW attribute. These nodes have the form:

^$W[INDOW] ( wname , gkeyword , gname , dkeyword , drawnum )

gname is the identifier for a gadget and wname is the identifier for the window that is the parent of that gadget.

    dkeyword::= expr V DRAW
    drawnum::= numexpr
