9.5.27 Applicability of Event Information Attributes

The applicability of einfoattribute nodes to etypes and betypes is specified by Table 5. A "Yes" entry indicates that the einfoattribute is defined for the etype or betype. An empty entry indicates that the einfoattribute is reserved for the etype or betype. implementationattribute values are applicable to all etypes and betypes.
